About Me

Hi, I'm Madhumitha, a 19 y/o pursuing a bachelor's degree in Information Technology and I'm from Chennai. I love the Harry Potter series and spend a lot of my free time reading theories and headcannons about the books.

I also love to write and draw. Hope you're able to view this picture of Harry, drawn by me. So this is my pensieve, a collection of perspectives I wrote from the POVs of certain characters. I hope you enjoy them!
